How does it work?

Breathee is your personal stable breathing assistant. It's role is to help you unwind and calm down, using calming breathing techniques and patterns. With guided breathing including visual helps, it shifts your focus, allowing to reduce momental anxiety and improve mindfulness.


When it's not enough
